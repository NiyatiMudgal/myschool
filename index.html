The School

Education is a social vehicle, which must forge ahead and be a source of new ideas. The school thus endeavours to provide the right opportunities and environment for the harmonious development of the personality of the student by giving a thorough and general education and also conserving the cultural heritage of the country. The School Emblem depicts "GODDESS SARASWATI" the Goddess of Wisdom and Knowledge . Our motto "Wisdom Is Glory" clearly highlights the mission of our school, i.e. to spread the light of Wisdom. We believe in developing the character, confidence and creativity of students, so that they are equipped to deal with any situation they come across. As such, we are deeply committed to the teaching learning process, which is a part of every school. Our mission is to nurture each child, just as a sapling is nurtured to enable it to grow into a strong and healthy tree. With the right foundation, we are sure that our students will grow up to be confident young individuals, the truly global citizens of tomorrow. School Rules

1) All students of the school are expected to maintain a high standard of discipline and behaviour, both inside and outside the school premises and in pursuance of that, the following rules have been laid down.

2) Students should reach school fifteen minutes before the school bell rings for Assembly. Late comers, improperly dressed students or any other kind of defaulter will be fined or would be sent back home at the risk of parent/guardian.

3) Students should be polite wherever they are. Shouting and whistling is not permitted in the school building, bus or in the campus.

4) School is not responsible for goods or money lost. Hence, parents should not give valuable articles like watches, gold ornaments or fancy articles to their wards.

5) Parents are not allowed to visit students or teachers in classrooms without the permission of the Principal or the MD.

6) For birthday celebrations, students are allowed to bring only standard quality of toffees. No other kind of preparation/sweets or drinks are allowed.

7)-a) Tiffins should be brought for lunch. Purchase of eatables from vendors outside the school is strictly prohibited.

b) Attendance of a pupil should not be less than 90%. Absence from school should be sought in writing well in time.

c) Proper medical certificate should be submitted in case of sick leave.
